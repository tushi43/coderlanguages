Learning sites

I would like to recommend

There is lot of things available on the internet to learn any programming paradigm or Language. One of the best ways to learn is through reading a book and understanding then applying the knowledge. You can check was best suits you. There are plenty of material available for learning. At minimal level you have three ways to learn

  1. Books

    2. Books are one of the best thing to approach while studing because it helps with syntax as well as with knowlege about that particluar snippet of code, it makes you understand usage of that code with any examples, I have referred books for java and C which was very helpful.

  2. Documentation

    3. When the technology/language is new in market and trending, you will not find any Videos instantly so referring to documentation helps, it also helps to explore all the great functions the language/framework possess. I have used documentation while learning PHP and Bootstrap. It also helps getting errors solved and also gives alternate methods/functions.

  3. Video Tutorials (You can refer YouTube/Udemy/Coursera)

    4. This is the one of best and easy method to learn any language but the problem is not every aspect is explained some little things are left behind. It's interactive and probably the best way to learn core concepts by experienced faculty.

These are some of the sites which have many good content to learn and understand better.

Coursera

On coursera you will get all type of courses related to programming, design and many others. There are about 2,000 courses available on coursera in different fields and many good ones are of data science. Coursera works with universities and other organizations to offer online courses, specializations, and degrees in a variety of subjects. You need to pay a ransom amount of money to enroll for a course.

 Udemy

Udemy is yet another good platform for learning through courses .It provides tools which enable users to create a course, promote it and earn money from student tuition charges. A important thing is no Udemy courses are currently credentialed for college credit, students take courses largely as a means of improving job-related skills. Some other good sites are Udacity, Khan Academy, edX, Lynda.com

 GeeksforGeeks

GeeksforGeeks is the Ultimate saviour for you if you want to learn and practise various problems. If you are a computer engineer you will need to visit the site almost day. It has the best collection of basic problems and simple solutions to them. I ws developed by some students of IIT Roorkee. It has various categories and also youtube channel for learning. The interview section also has the most asked questions with answers with quizzes and many more. Do explore the site!!

 w3schools

If you want to start with web development,w3schools is the best in terms of syntax as well as their detailed meaning. Even many teachers refers to this site for teaching their students and I had also used this site when building this website. It is the world's largest web developer site. The inbuilt interpreter is very well design and you can try some different cde within that interpreter. The SQL is also precisely explained with all the major DBMS like SQL-Server, MySQL, Oracle DB and more. The bootstrap framework is also brilliantly explained with examples.

 Codecademy

While the above sites have coding courses as their part, codecademy is entire website dedicated to coding website. There are free as well as paid courses. Codecademy is an online freemium interactive platform that offers free coding classes in 12 different programming languages. The site also offers a paid "pro" option that gives users access to a personalized learning plan, quizzes, realistic projects, and live help from advisors

 StackOverflow

StackOverflow is a one spot solution to all your errors. Whenever you are struck somewhere during any software installation or any logic error or any other thing bothering you just search in google most of the time you'll get answer on stackoverflow. It features questions and answers on a wide range of topics in computer programming. The website serves as a platform for users to ask and answer questions, and, through membership and active participation, to vote questions and answers up or down and edit questions and answers. Many experienced developers answer to the questions in detail which helps in improving concept as well as solving error. Do make a account on this site and also help if you can!

 StackExchange

Stack Exchange is a network of question-and-answer websites on topics in varied fields, each site covering a specific topic, where questions, answers, and users are subject to a reputation award process. StackExchange is more about conceptual knowledge, about technical papers and many more.

 YouTube

YouTube is the best place to find study material for anything, the reason is it's free and you must subscribe to channel. Just search for the topic and there is a series of videos often labelled as Tutorials series by various youtube channels. There are many good channels like thenewboston, codedamn, Traversy, DerekBanas, KhanAcademy, and more. My favourite is the thenewboston.
